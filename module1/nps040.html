Lesson 1: Introduction to Housing Management

Accessing iQMIS

Page 1 of 14

Depending on your need in iQMIS, your permission will be Housing Managers, Tenant Managers or Read Only Managers. Users and their supervisor will complete and sign form and send to the WASO Housing Management Office.

While having all this information available to us in the database, it is good practice to keep a copy of the paperwork associated with each tenant, especially those that require signatures. These documents need to be safeguarded and kept secure.
