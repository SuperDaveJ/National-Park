NPS Business Principles and Practices Fundamentals

Lesson 1: Introduction to Housing Management

Lesson Overview

Page 1 of 14

This lesson provides an overview of the housing policies, regulations and guidelines and the Internet Quarters Management Information System (iQMIS) automated inventory of government housing used by the National Park Service (NPS).

Upon completion of this lesson, you should be able to:

  • Describe the NPS policies, regulations and guidelines for administering and managing employee housing
  • Describe the various uses of the iQMIS
